## Prompt base de capitulo

Capitulo 9, "O Preco da Lealdade". The party in Elriste realizes they forgot payment, returns to the rich Alva Citadel, considers selling rebel information, wins more money in a noble gambling room, and fractures when Kaelion warns Rita. Tone shifts from chaotic table comedy to political betrayal. Use clear multi-panel webcomic pages, European fantasy graphic novel style, readable Portuguese speech bubbles only.
