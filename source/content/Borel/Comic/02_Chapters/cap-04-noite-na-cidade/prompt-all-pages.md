# Prompt All Pages - Cap. 4

## Prefixo Global

```
Generate an image. European fantasy graphic novel style, detailed digital illustration, inked influences, cel shading influences, muted earth and bronze palette, medieval fantasy, cinematic lighting, comic book art, clear silhouettes.
```

## Continuidade Global

Use the attached reference images for character identity and outfits. Tony Tigger is the human fighter-paladin with silver plate armor, white cloak red lining, lion shield and greatsword. Nightwolf is the dark elf drow ranger in humanoid ranger form only, charcoal skin, red eyes, olive cloak, bow and quiver. Dustin is the young human ranger-rogue with dark leather armor and ornate bow. Kaelion is the muscular elf warrior in dark samurai lamellar armor with two katanas. Bartrock is the resurrected normal elven mage, gaunt pale, no mask, no corruption, no smoke. Emmergard is a blonde Germanic warrior-tavern keeper woman with a compact powerful build, broad shoulders, muscular worker arms, practical dark leather over green tunic, burgundy cloak/scarf, heavy spiked mace, braided blonde hair, and a friendly confident expression; her blonde hair, grounded strength, non-glamorous tavern-warrior look, and strong compact silhouette are mandatory. The hostile werewolf creature is not Nightwolf, has no cloak, no bow, no quiver.
