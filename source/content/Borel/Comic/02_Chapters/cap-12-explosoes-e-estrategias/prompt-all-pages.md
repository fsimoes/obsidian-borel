# Prompt All Pages - Cap. 12

## Base fixa

```text
Generate an image. European fantasy graphic novel style, detailed digital illustration, inked influences, cel shading influences, muted earth and bronze palette, medieval fantasy, cinematic lighting, comic book art, clear silhouettes.

Use the attached character references for Tony Tigger, Nightwolf, Dustin, Bartrock, Borin and Trash. Keep faces, body shapes, outfits and weapons consistent. Rita Dente de Bronze is a fierce red-haired revolutionary leader in weathered light leather armor with a visible bronze tooth when she smiles. The Crystal Guards wear pale ornate medieval armor with translucent crystal plates and cold blue inner glow.
Portuguese speech bubbles only when specified. Use exact dialogue text from the page prompt. Clear comic page layout with readable panel order.

Avoid: photorealistic, anime, 3d render, watermark, logo, blurry, extra fingers, extra arms, deformed hands, bad anatomy, English text unless specified, gibberish letters, illegible font, misspelled Portuguese.
```
