# Prompts — Cap. 1

> One image-generation prompt per page. **10 pages** · multi-panel vertical webcomic (~1400px wide).  
> Sources: [[panels|panels.md]] · [[dialogues|dialogues.md]] · [[script|script.md]] · [[style-tirinha|style-tirinha.md]] · [[prompt-all-pages-tirinha|prompt-all-pages-tirinha.md]]

## Style baseline

**Graphic style:** Fantasy RPG webcomic — Borel approved funny look (Jun 2026 v2). Clean bold black line art, professional cel-shading, warm readable palette (wood browns, amber torchlight, deep blues at night — never muddy or grim). Exaggerated facial expressions, clear comedic body language, slapstick-friendly staging. Modern western comic panels with subtle manga influence. Warm candlelit medieval tavern interior when indoors.

**Format:** One vertical page image per output. Multiple sub-panels per page with clear black gutters. Read sub-panels top-left to bottom-right. ~1400px wide.

**Speech:** Clear round speech bubbles with tails. Legible hand-lettered comic font. Portuguese dialogue **exactly** as quoted (colloquial Brazilian Portuguese). No English text.

**Style refs (attach in order, every generation):**
1. `Referencias/style/cap-01-estilo-aprovado-dustin-traidor.png`
2. `Referencias/style/cap-01-estilo-aprovado-gola.png`
3. `Referencias/style/cap-v3-humor-piloto.png`

**Local copies:** `refs/01-estilo-dustin-traidor.png` · `refs/02-estilo-gola.png` (see [[refs/README|refs/README.md]])

**Character refs:**
| ID | Path | Character |
|----|------|-----------|
| A | `Referencias/pcs/bartrock-noble-eq-inicial.png` | Lord Bart — young tall slim elven noble mage, messy dark hair, pointed ears, fine dark coat, wand, **no demon mask** |
| B | `Referencias/pcs/nightwolf-eq-inicial.png` | Nightwolf — drow ranger, charcoal-black skin, glowing red eyes, long black hair, thin scar through left eye, olive-green hooded cloak, wooden recurve bow — **humanoid ranger only, not werewolf** |
| C | `Referencias/pcs/dustin-eq-inicial.png` | Dustin — lean human ranger-rogue, dark brown leather armor, ornate recurve bow, **dual daggers on belt** (not longsword), green gem ring |
| D | `Referencias/npcs/tobias-peso-morto-eq-inicial.png` | Tobias Peso Morto — halfling ~1.20 m, teal scarf, brown jerkin, **traitor** |

**Avoid:** photorealistic, hyper-detailed oil painting, dark muddy palette, grim cinematic lighting, ultra-flat newspaper strip, anime chibi, 3D render, watermark, logo, blurry, extra fingers, extra arms, deformed hands, bad anatomy, English text, gibberish letters, illegible font, text outside bubbles, misspelled Portuguese, werewolf Nightwolf, Bartrock demon mask, wrong character faces, red-haired Emmergard, Dustin with longsword, Tobias same height as elf without arm stretched up, Tobias portrayed as scam victim, Lord Bart attacking guard with magic before brawl, dedicated open-palm slap panel, SFX "TAP!" on Bart's face.

**Canon order (do not invert):** Payment dispute → Tobias **denounces** trio to guards → captain questions Bart → Bart insults (words only) → tavern **chaos** (no slap panel) → brawl → Tobias on guards' side, **collar grab** (halfling low × elf tall) → Bart falls → Nightwolf + Dustin **carry** Bart and flee.

**Setting:** Night, Rilonde. Tavern *O Cálice Dourado* / Golden Cup — warm windows, cobblestone streets, wood interior, candles.
